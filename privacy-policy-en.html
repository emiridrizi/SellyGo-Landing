Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Introduction

PazarGoo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information
  • Phone Numbers: We collect phone numbers for SMS verification via AWS SNS for user registration, guest product posting, and password reset functionality.
  • Payment Information: We process payment information through Stripe PaymentSheet for product boosting services (pinning on Home page, Product Category, or Location). Prices in MKD are converted to EUR using real-time exchange rates with no hidden fees.
  • Account Details: We store user account information including email addresses in our secure database.
2.2 User-Generated Content
  • Product listings, descriptions, and images that you post to our platform
  • Communications and interactions with other users

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Phone Numbers: Used exclusively for SMS verification during registration, guest posting, and password reset processes.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe for product boosting services only.
  • Account Details: Used to provide app functionality, manage your account, and personalize your experience.
  • User-Generated Content: Displayed on our platform to facilitate buying and selling transactions.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in our secure database with encryption
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with the following third-party services:

  • AWS SNS: For SMS verification services
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Currency Conversion API: For real-time MKD to EUR conversion

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our system
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request information about how your data is processed

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Apple App Store Guideline 5.1.1
  • Google Play Store Data Safety requirements
  • Stripe's legal requirements

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
